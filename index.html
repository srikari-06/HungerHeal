Our Process

Food Donation

Restaurants, grocery stores, and individuals register surplus food donations through our system.

Quality Check

Our team verifies the quality and safety of donated food items before redistribution.

Matching & Logistics

The system matches donations with nearby organizations in need and optimizes pickup routes.

Distribution

Food is distributed to shelters, food banks, and community centers serving those in need.
